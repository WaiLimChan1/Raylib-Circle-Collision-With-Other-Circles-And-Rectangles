Simulation Description

	Simulates perfect collision between circle VS circle and circle VS rectangle.
	However, it doesn't handle rectangle movement. 



Keybinds

	R - Restart

	Y - Spawn A Circle
	U - Spawn A Box

	W,A,S,D - Manual Control Over Circle Movement

	T - Give All Circles Random Velocity

	Left Mouse Button - All Circles Move To Mouse
	Right Mouse Button - All Circles Move Away From Mouse

	F - Toggle Circle Collision Lines
	G - Toggle Box Collision Lines
	H - Toggle Velocity Lines
